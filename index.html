SafeRing

Smart Personal Safety Ring for Children, Women & Elderly

What is SafeRing?

SafeRing is a screen-free smart safety ring designed to keep families connected and protected. It helps parents track their children safely and allows working adults to monitor the well-being of their elderly parents — without screens, games, or distractions.

SafeRing works quietly in the background and sends important alerts directly to the caregiver’s mobile.
